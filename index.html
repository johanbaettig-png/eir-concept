About Eir

A new kind of
insurance company.

We were founded in Stockholm in 2019 with one conviction: that insurance infrastructure should be digital, flexible, and built for the partners who distribute it — not the other way around.

We are fully licensed by the Swedish FSA, entirely cloud-native, and built from the ground up to serve distribution partners who want to offer insurance without losing control of their customer relationships.
